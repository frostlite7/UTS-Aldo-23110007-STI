ITBSS.

We bring a renewal, the best standard of a Technology and Business Institute in Pontianak, West Kalimantan, with a noble vision and mission, moving forward for the progress of the nation's children through the Sabda Setia Institute of Technology and Business.

The Sabda Setia Institute of Technology and Business was founded in 2021 to encourage the nation's progress to the global level. With the spirit of renewal in our vision and mission, ITBSS offers broad access to various career opportunities. As an institute that brings new standards, ITBSS dares to develop to become the best in its field.

History

Driven by a strong desire and desire to participate in the process of national intelligence and is a continuation of the secondary education level which has been organized by the Kampung Bali Protestant Church Foundation (YGPKB), which is an extension of the service mandated by the West Kalimantan Christian Church (GKKB) Pontianak Congregation in in the field of education, and with the spirit of nationalism and Indonesian unity on October 13 2019, the Higher Education Compartment (KomDikti) was formed by YGPKB Pontianak with the aim of being the organizer of establishing higher education called the Sabda Setia Institute of Technology and Business (ITBSS).
