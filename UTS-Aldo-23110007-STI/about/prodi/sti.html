Information Systems and Technology Study Program (S1)

The Information Systems and Technology (STI) study program is a combination of Information Systems (SI) and Information Technology (IT) to answer the challenges of increasingly rapid technological development and digital transformation. STI learns how to design and implement digital products (applications, websites, and designs) that suit business goals and solve everyday life problems. STI also studies emerging technologies.

Vision

To become a superior study program in the field of Information Systems and Technology, especially in software engineering, as well as contributing to the development of new technology based on Christian values ​​and a technopreneur spirit.

Mission

  1. Organizing education and teaching so as to produce quality graduates in the field of information systems and technology that meet the needs of society and industry.

  2. Carrying out research to develop quality science and technology.

  3. Organizing community service activities in the field of Information Systems and Technology.

  4. Establishing cooperation between the Information Systems and Technology study program and related institutions and industries, nationally and internationally.

  5. Fostering the character of students by instilling Christian values, integrity, work ethic, and sense of responsibility, so that they are equipped to become successful and contributing members of society.

Goals

  1. Producing graduates with technopreneurship spirit who utilize information systems and technology to innovate and solve real-world problems.

  2. Preparing graduates to become innovative and user-centered technologists who can develop and implement technological solutions that are effective, efficient, and tailored to industry needs.

  3. Preparing graduates with the necessary competencies to carry out their job duties effectively and efficiently, in accordance with industry needs.

  4. Increase research activities in the field of information systems and technology as a contribution to technological development.

  5. Improving the results and impact of community service in the field of information systems and technology that is beneficial to society.

Objective

  1. Achieving excellence in the implementation of education so that graduates are ready to compete globally.

  2. The implementation of effective and quality learning in the field of Information Systems and Technology, both in theory and practice.

  3. The implementation of cooperation with various agencies or institutions and industries that are relevant to the expertise of study programs in the country and abroad.

  4. The formation of the character of the academic community who are able to apply work ethic and Christian values in practice in the world of work.

Profile of Graduates

  1. Graduates are able to act and judge professionally.

  2. Graduates are able to think logically, critically and systematically in utilizing informatics/computer science knowledge to solve real problems.

  3. Graduates have the ability to analyze, design, create, and evaluate information systems that are aligned with organizational goals.

  4. Graduates are able to develop multi-platform based technology products.

  5. Graduates are able to apply knowledge in the Programming and Software Development function area in their profession.

Graduate Career Prospects

  1. Augmented & Virtual Reality Developer

  2. Data Analysts & Scientists

  3. Digital Transformation Specialists

  4. Internet of Things Specialist

  5. IT Project Manager

  6. Network Security Analyst

  7. Software Developer

  8. UI/UX Designer
